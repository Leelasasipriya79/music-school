Vocal & Instrumental Music Classes in Karwar – Shri Ram Music Class

At Shri Ram Music Class, our mission is to nurture students' creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration skills through engaging musical experiences that are rooted in tradition and adapted to modern learning needs. We offer a range of certified music courses in Vocal Music (Hindustani Classical and Semi-Classical), Harmonium, Keyboard, and Music Theory. Whether you're a beginner or aspiring performer, our programs are designed to guide students in building a strong foundation in both the practical and theoretical aspects of music. Our classes are available in in-person, online, and hybrid formats to ensure flexible learning options for students of all ages and skill levels. We are proud to be affiliated with esteemed institutions like Pracheen Kala Kendra and Rabindra Bharati University, ensuring that every learner receives structured and recognized training from dedicated instructors. Join Shri Ram Music Class — where “Crafting Musicians for Tomorrow” isn't just our tagline, but our promise to every student.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To nurture students' creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration skills through engaging musical experiences that blend traditional depth with modern approaches.

🌟 Vision

To become a leading institution in India for classical and contemporary music education, shaping passionate learners into confident, lifelong musicians.

About Shri Ram Music Class

Shri Ram Music Class is a dedicated center for learning Indian classical and semi-classical music in Karwar. With a vision to preserve and promote the rich heritage of Indian music, we provide structured training in vocal music, harmonium, keyboard, and music theory.

Rooted in the traditional Guru-Shishya Parampara and empowered by modern teaching methodologies, our classes are designed to help students of all ages discover the joy of music while developing discipline, creativity, and confidence.

Affiliated with prestigious institutions like Pracheen Kala Kendra and Rabindra Bharati University, we offer certified courses and a nurturing environment where students grow both musically and personally. Each note learned here is a step toward harmony, self-expression, and inner peace.

LEGENDARY MAESTROS WHO INSPIRE SHRI RAM MUSIC CLASS

Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia

A guiding light for aspiring flautists, his soulful renditions serve as a deep source of inspiration for our students at Shri Ram Music Class.

Pandit Rajan & Sajan Misra

This celebrated vocal duo reflects the essence of Hindustani tradition — their journey deeply influences the vocal training offered at our institute.

Pandit Subhankar Banerjee

Known for his command over rhythm, his tabla artistry inspires our rhythm sessions and enhances students' understanding of Taal and contemporary styles.

Pandit Pravin Godkhindi

A contemporary icon, his innovative style resonates with Shri Ram Music Class's mission to blend classical roots with modern expression.
