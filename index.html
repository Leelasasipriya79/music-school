About Shri Ram Music Class

Shri Ram Music Class is a premier destination for aspiring musicians in Karwar, dedicated to spreading the joy of music and nurturing talent at all levels. We specialize in Indian classical, semi-classical, and instrumental training including vocals, harmonium, keyboard, tabla, and music theory.

Rooted in the traditional Guru-Shishya Parampara, we combine timeless values with innovative teaching techniques. Our environment is student-centered, encouraging creativity, discipline, and emotional expression through music.

Affiliated with renowned institutions like Pracheen Kala Kendra and Rabindra Bharati University, we provide structured, certified courses that not only build musical skills but also confidence, focus, and cultural appreciation.
